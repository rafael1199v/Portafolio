Sobre mí

Since I was a child, technology has captivated me, especially software applications and how they work. Writing my first program in high school led me to study Software Engineering at Universidad Católica San Pablo, where I’ve been able to polish my skills in the art of software development.

I am a person with initiative, communicative and empathetic, who can adapt to environments that change quickly, with a remarkable work ethic. Furthermore, I enjoy expanding my knowledge and contributing to the field.

For me, software engineering is not just about programming languages and frameworks, it’s about analyzing and designing maintainable long-term applications to solve real-world problems.
